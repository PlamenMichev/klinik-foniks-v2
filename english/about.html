About Klinik Føniks

How it started

I always knew I wanted to work with people and nature. People who are struggling with finding themselves, who can never really seem to get a grasp of peace no matter what they do. I searched and I searched, and one day, it finally popped up: Heilpraktik. The more I read about it, the more I knew that this was it. This is my passion. I then started my studies in 2020 and here I am today. Ready to enrichen your life, to help you become the person you were always meant to be, free of pain.

Welcome to my clinic.

Certified Heilpraktiker

I believe that caring for human life through nature is vital. Our bodies are complex, and the only way to properly care about it is understanding it wholely and fully, to truly embrace ourselves. I want to open your eyes to how much life has to offer through a complete rebirth of your mind, soul and body.

I’m offering you a personalized plan where I will follow and support you through the process. It is a path you must be willing to take, but it is worth it in the end. Book a meeting today, so we can make a plan to pull out your deep-rooted problems.
