About Klinik Føniks

How it started

Alternative medicine, healing, heilpraktik, naturopathy, witchery - it goes by many names. The practice mainly stems from Germany and ancient Asia, places of development and traditionalism. The idea is to treat people, involving their whole person, with all-natural and spiritual ways - to truly identify the root cause and dig it out from the ground up.
